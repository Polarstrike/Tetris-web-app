Tetris Game

GAME RULES



Fill an entire row to make it disappear and gain points!
Pop multiple row at the same time to get bonus points!
Pieces will fall faster as you gather points!

HOW TO PLAY



Move the piece: LEFT and RIGHT ARROW KEYS
Rotate the piece: Q and W
Speed up falling speed: ARROW KEY DOWN
Start, Pause and UnPause the game: P

ACCOUNT SYSTEM



Register to save your best scores and see your personal highscores!
You can Register by entering your Username, Email and Password.
You can Login once registered by entering your Username and your Password.

MANAGEMENT SYSTEM



There is an Account Management Tool reserved to the System admin.
You can access it by logging in as Admin (Username: admin)
